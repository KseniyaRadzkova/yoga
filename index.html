Everyone's yoga

We offers over 4,000 professionally-filmed online yoga classes taught by experienced, certified yoga teachers to customers around the world. We believe that yoga should be accessible to everyone and our classes are designed to give you an in-studio experience, at an affordable price, in your own home.

Balance body & mind

Yoga strengthens the overall mind-body connection and helps you deal more effectively with unpleasant emotions.

Weight loss

An everiday gentle yoga practice will fuel the metabolic system and will help burn fat, leading to weight loss.

Meditation practice

Meditating for just a few minutes in the morning can result in better concentration throughout the day.

Yoga is a way to freedom

You will feel more calm and relaxed, and less stressed and anxious. You will experience more self-confidence and self-acceptance.

Increased energy

Daily yoga practice will awaken the main energy centres (called chakras) in your body.
